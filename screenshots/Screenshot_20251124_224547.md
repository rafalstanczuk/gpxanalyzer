# Map with Elevation Profile

Detailed map view showing your GPS track overlaid on OpenStreetMap with "POWERED BY STRAVA" integration. The route is displayed as an orange line tracing your path, with a green pin marker indicating the current point of interest. Below the map, an elevation profile chart visualizes altitude changes with color-coded segments - green for ascents and red for descents. The vertical axis shows elevation in meters, and the chart automatically highlights key elevation points along your route.
