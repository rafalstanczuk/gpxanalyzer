# Side-by-Side Map & Elevation View

View your route and elevation profile simultaneously in a horizontal layout that gives you a clearer, more comprehensive view of your activity. See your GPS track on the map alongside your elevation chart so you can instantly understand how the terrain you're seeing on the map relates to the climbs and descents shown in the chart. The synchronized selection lets you tap any point to see the exact location on the map that matches the elevation data, making it easier to analyze your performance.
