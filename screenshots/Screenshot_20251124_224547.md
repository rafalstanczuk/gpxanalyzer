# Strava-Powered Map with Elevation Chart

Map view showing your activity route as an orange GPS track line overlaid on OpenStreetMap terrain. Green pin marker with thumbs-up icon indicates current location. "POWERED BY STRAVA" logo displayed prominently. Map displays Polish landmarks and locations. Below the map, elevation profile chart visualizes altitude from 147m to 224m with color-coded segments: green for uphill sections and red for downhill sections. The chart provides visual representation of terrain difficulty throughout your activity route.
