# Split-Screen Map & Elevation View

Left panel displays map with your GPS track shown as orange and blue lines overlaid on OpenStreetMap. Green pin marker indicates current location. "POWERED BY STRAVA" branding visible. Map shows detailed terrain with landmarks, roads, and geographic features. Right panel shows elevation profile chart with colored background zones (light blue, light purple, light yellow, light pink) representing different elevation ranges. Chart displays altitude from 147m to 224m with green areas for ascents and red for descents. Vertical dashed line and tooltip show synchronized point selection between map and chart.
